Syllabus of the Information Retrieval course

Summary

The goal of this course is to introduce the student to a part of the wide area of information retrieval, namely the search of text patterns into texts. This approach allows to avoid the complexity of mathematical models and focus on a more computer science topic: character strings and finite automata. After the course, the students should be able to write different kind of algorithms and corresponding C programs to search efficiently words in texts.

Prerequisites

None.

Textbook

This course does not require any book in particular, as the focus is mainly in doing lots of short homeworks.

Evaluation

Two examinations as usual, plus homeworks twice a week. Exercises will be solved during class-time and answers posted on the web site. Attendance will be checked and taken into account for the final grade.
